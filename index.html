Notes

Rates are from 協会けんぽ (Japan Health Insurance Association) fiscal year 2024 (April). The 標準報酬月額 (standard monthly remuneration) is looked up from the official 50-grade table. 厚生年金 (Kosei Nenkin) is capped at ¥650,000. 介護保険 (care insurance, 1.60% total) applies to employees aged 40–64. Employee and employer each bear half the total premium. This calculator assumes 協会けんぽ membership — company-specific health insurance societies (健保組合) may differ.
